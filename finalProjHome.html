Here at Green Bean Coffee Co. we share a passion for coffee and houseplants, striving to connect the indoors with the outdoors and bring a sense of community. Using different beans from all around the globe, and houseplants derived from many parts of the world as well.

We partner with family owned farms across the globe to bring you the highest quality ethically sourced coffee that we can provide. Every week we update and add to our collection of houseplants, and daily make sure to have a coffee bean that suits your pallet.

We have plant specialists and coffee connoisseurs here to offer guidance and answer any questions you may have. All are welcome, come have a chat, study, or lounge in our pet-friendly green space.
